About Me

As an innovative data scientist and machine learning engineer, I lead teams to craft scalable analytics solutions that turn data into strategic insights. With over seven years of experience building end‑to‑end pipelines in cloud environments, I specialize in geospatial analytics, large language model fine‑tuning and MLOps. My passion lies in transforming complex datasets into actionable intelligence that drives business impact.

My work has resulted in up to 40% reductions in cloud spend, 25% increases in data pipeline throughput and award‑winning solutions for clients across industries.
