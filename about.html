About Me

The researcher, engineer, and leader behind the models.

Biography

Principal Data Scientist & ML Engineer

I am a passionate data scientist who thrives at the intersection of technology and business strategy. As Principal Data Scientist at Mogean, I own the company's Geospatial Data Solutions — leading a team of data scientists and engineers to design the data lifecycle and build production-grade ML models from multi-terabyte geospatial datasets.

My journey began with a Master's in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Georgia Tech, where I built a rigorous foundation in signal processing and machine learning. Over 7+ years at Mogean I have grown from the first data scientist hired to the principal role, building proof-of-concept solutions that evolved into core products and driving efficiency increases of up to 40%.

I am also deeply engaged with the AI community — from competing in hackathons and prompt engineering competitions to building custom small language models, volunteering with Statistics Without Borders, and speaking on podcasts about MLOps and reducing bias in machine learning.

Current Focus: Agentic coding, context engineering, LLM fine-tuning (Deepseek, Llama3), building evals for AI agents, and designing production-grade natural language query interfaces.
