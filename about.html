About Me

Biography

I am a passionate data scientist who thrives at the intersection of technology and business strategy. As Chief Data Scientist at Mogean, I lead a talented team that harnesses geospatial and behavioral data to create customer insights. My journey began with a Master’s degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Georgia Tech, where I cultivated a rigorous foundation in signal processing and machine learning. Since then I have accumulated over eight years of experience transforming raw datasets into actionable intelligence across several industries.

At Mogean I am responsible for designing and deploying cloud‑native pipelines, fine‑tuning large language models for niche use cases and crafting real‑time dashboards. I am bilingual in English and Hindi and hold certifications across Amazon Web Services and machine learning specialties. Outside of work I enjoy volunteering with Statistics Without Borders and guest speaking on podcasts about MLOps, reducing bias in ML and bridging the academic‑industry gap.
